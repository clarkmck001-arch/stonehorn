Our Story

Stonehorn started with a sketch. Now it is a movement worn in the wild.

This page is the build log behind the brand: where it started, what it stands for, and how each drop earned its place.

Origin

Stonehorn came from one question: what does your hat say first?

The first Stonehorn caps were sketches on old receipts, stitched into reality after late nights and test runs. Every panel, curve, and logo hit was tuned for one thing: presence.
